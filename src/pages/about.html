About Us

AvengeTech is an ever-growing family friendly Minecraft community. We focus on providing a safe and fun environment for players of all ages. Our community is committed to upholding values of respect, kindness, and inclusivity. We currently offer a variety of game modes and experiences, including the original Prison gamemode and a constantly updating SkyBlock experience!

Founded by Sn3ak in October 2015, the network began with its first iteration, AT v1, featuring the Factions gamemode. This version lasted for nearly two years before Sn3ak decided to shut it down to rewrite and improve the server. In the summer of 2017, AT v2 was launched, introducing KitPvP and Prison gamemodes. These gamemodes brought new excitement and challenges to the community, but in late July 2018, KitPvP was permanently retired. SkyBlock was introduced in early August 2018, replacing KitPvP and quickly becoming a fan favorite. The network continued to thrive with SkyBlock and Prison gamemodes until early February 2024, when it was shut down once again for a major overhaul. During the summer of 2023, Kally, Pringz, and Miguel joined the team, bringing fresh ideas and expertise to the design and development of the network. Their contributions were instrumental in shaping the next iteration of AvengeTech. AT v3 launched in June 2024, featuring a revamped version of the SkyBlock gamemode, with Kally and Miguel leading the development efforts. Prison was relaunched in August 2024, further solidifying the network's commitment to providing engaging and innovative gameplay experiences. With Kally and Miguel at the helm of development and Pringz managing the staff and community, AvengeTech has continued to grow and evolve, offering players a welcoming and dynamic environment to explore and enjoy.
